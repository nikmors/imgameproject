IMGameProject:


IMGameProject is a vision project created by Nick Moraitis, an individual developer to introduce his work and his passion for gaming. In this website you will find a whole portofolio of his game projects.

"Give birth to ideas...
Develop your ideas...
Play your ideas!"

Nick Moraitis: Software engineer/ Game developer

Focus On: (click here)
All the games are built for PC, Mac and Linux Standalone. Our commitment is that people will have the chance to enjoy all the games from IMGameProject on the Web, on their Android Mobile phones, iPhones and Tablets!
The long-term goal for this game website project is to become a "house of games" that users will have the chance to upload their demo projects with no costs and show their work and share their true love of gaming.
