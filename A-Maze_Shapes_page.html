A-Maze Shapes Game






Introduction:


A puzzle game developed in Unity3D game engine.


Details:


Main players are 3D shapes trying to find their way out from mazes full of traps and time pressure. The player has to find certain switches in the game and solve loads of puzzles to open/close gates, switch off laser beams that keeps him away from the right paths, disarm traps that stops his way out and travel through portals. But he has to be fast enough before the time ends and the mechanical device inside every shape explodes!
Don't hesitate then! Find your way out as soon as possible...Download the game and see if you can make it!



Android game changes:


The players are able to control the characters in the game by the advantage of motion sensors that iphones and android devices can provide. Instead of pressing keys on your phone you can swing it, rotate it, tilt it or even shake it to guide the characters into the game. A-Maze Shapes game uses the Accelerometer input to have control over your shapes (main characters) as described above and why not adding more fun into the game itself! Enjoy!!
